Notes and Probing Questions








Probing Questions

Situation

  • What shows on the device screen when you have the problem?
  • Do you get any error messages?
  • What were you doing with the device when this started happening?
  • Which device is having the issue? Are other devices also having the issue?
  • For Dual SIM with eSIM devices, which line is having the problem?

Duration

  • When did you first notice the issue?
  • Has anything changed recently?
  • How often does it happen?
  • Can you repeat the same steps and always have the same problem, or is it sporadic?

Location

  • Do you see the issue occuring at a specific place?
  • How many bars of signal do you have? Do the signal bars fluctuate?
  • Do you see 3G/4G/LTE/5G on your screen?
  • Are you inside or outside?
